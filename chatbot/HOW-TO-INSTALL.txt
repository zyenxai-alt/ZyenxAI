HOW TO INSTALL YOUR AI CHATBOT
================================
Built for: Zyenx AI (Internal)
By: Zyenx AI Agency
zyenxai.com | hello@zyenxai.com

========================================
STEP 1 — Add your Anthropic API key
========================================
1. Copy .env.example to .env
2. Add your key: ANTHROPIC_API_KEY=sk-ant-your-key-here
3. Get key from: console.anthropic.com

========================================
STEP 2 — Deploy Chatbot Server (Railway)
========================================
1. Go to railway.app — free account
2. New Project → Deploy
3. Upload this chatbot folder
4. Add environment variables from .env
5. Deploy — get URL like: https://abc.railway.app

========================================
STEP 3 — Update widget.js
========================================
Open widget.js and find line:
  apiUrl: 'https://your-chatbot-server.railway.app/chat'
Replace with your Railway URL:
  apiUrl: 'https://abc.railway.app/chat'

========================================
STEP 4 — Add to your website
========================================
Add this ONE line before </body> in every page:

  <script src="https://abc.railway.app/widget.js"></script>

Replace abc.railway.app with your Railway URL.

========================================
STEP 5 — Done!
========================================
The chat bubble appears bottom-right of your website.
Visitors can ask questions 24/7.
Bot knows all about your business automatically.
